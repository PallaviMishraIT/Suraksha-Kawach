About Suraksha Kawach

Suraksha Kawach is dedicated to ensuring women's safety and security through innovative technology and community support. Our mission is to empower women by providing real-time safety solutions, helpline support, and safe zones.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every woman feels safe and protected, no matter where she is. By leveraging technology and public awareness, we strive to create a safer environment for all.


OBJECTIVES

The objective of Suraksha Kawach is to provide women with safety and empowerment through protection measures, accessible services, and technological support. Our mission focuses on both prevention and response to ensure holistic safety.

01

Provide immediate and comprehensive support to women affected by violence and distress.

02

Establish quality mechanisms for protection, rescue, and rehabilitation for women in need.

03

Enhance accessibility to government and safety services for women at various levels.

04

Strengthen capacity building and training of officials under different safety schemes.
